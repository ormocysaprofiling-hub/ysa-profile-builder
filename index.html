Data Privacy Notice

By submitting this form, you agree that the information and photo you provide — including your name, birthdate, contact details, address, church calling, and personal background — will be collected and processed by the Ormoc Philippine Stake YSA committee, in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

This information will be stored in a restricted Google Drive/Sheet accessible only to authorized stake and ward leaders, and used solely for ministering, member records, and YSA activity coordination. It will not be shared publicly or with third parties outside the stake.

You may request to have your information corrected or removed at any time by contacting the stake office at ormocysaprofiling@gmail.com.
